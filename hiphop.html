    Hip-Hop/Rap Opinions

    THIS IS MY OPINION. CHANCES ARE YOU WILL DISAGREE

    Fellas In Paris - Kanye West & Jay-Z

    9/10. Good vocals, great beat. All around good.

    True 2 Myself - Lil Tjay

    10/10 Amazing Songs. Great Debut Album. My Favourite Album Of All Time.

    Astro World - Travis Scott

    8/10. Solid album. Good Production. Great For Concerts (sometimes).

    Mr Morale & The Big Steppers - Kendrick Lamar

    9.5/10. Great songs. Deep message. Worth the 5 year wait to an extent.

    IGOR - Tyler, The Creator

    8/10. Not every song is good, but its still got some good songs. - Caleb

    Hall of Fame 2.0 - Polo G

    7/10. Some really good songs such as Bloody Canvas but most other songs aren't great.
